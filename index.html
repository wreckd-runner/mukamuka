About the race

The Mukamuka Munter is a classic Wellington trail run tracing the ridgelines and valleys of the Remutaka Range. Starting from the Hutt Valley floor, the course climbs sharply into native bush before threading across open tops with views of the Kāpiti Coast and Wairarapa. A proper lung-burner with enough technical terrain to keep things honest. No frills. Just trail.

Route guide
0 km Start — Hutt Valley trailhead. Follow the track markers onto Mukamuka Road.
4 km First major climb begins. 400m of vertical through dense bush. Stay on the main ridge.
10 km Ridgeline traverse. Open tussock and exposed tops — weather can change fast up here.
16 km Descent into the valley crossing. Creek ford — expect wet feet.
22 km Second climb. Shorter but steeper. Use the ropes if provided.
28 km Final descent into the finish. Marshals will guide you in from the road gate.
~30 km Finish. Recover. Eat something.
