MUKAMUKA MUNTER
Classic Wellington Trail Run Since 2007
About the race

The Mukamuka Munter is a classic Wellington trail run tracing the ridgelines and valleys of the Remutaka Range. Starting from the Hutt Valley floor, climbing sharply into native bush, across exposed tops with views of Kāpiti Coast and Wairarapa. Just trail, no frills.
