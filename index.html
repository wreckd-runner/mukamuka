The Race

Gnarly.
Raw.
Unmissable.

The Mukamuka Munter starts where the Orongorongo River meets the sea and ends in the Catchpool Valley — passing through everything Wellington's wilderness has to throw at you.

Coast, canyon, summit, forest. You'll be wet, you'll be tired, and you'll want to do it again the very next day.

The Route

Start at the Orongorongo River mouth. Head east along the wild South Wairarapa coastline for 12km of big surf and karaka trees.

Enter the canyon-like Mukamuka Stream valley — rock-hopping, river crossings, narrowing walls. Climb steeply to South Saddle.

Descend Mt Matthews Track to the Orongorongo River. Cross to Big Bend Track. Follow Catchpool Stream west to the finish.

This is NOT a fully marked course. Marshals and DOC triangles are at key points. Bring a map.

Mandatory & Recommended Gear

Come Prepared.

Gear is checked before your race pack is issued. There is no phone coverage for large sections. The Orongorongo River is deadly when high. This is a real backcountry environment.

REQThermal top & bottoms (polypropylene)
REQWaterproof jacket, full length
REQTopo map — 1:50,000 covering full route
REQCompass
REQEmergency food — 500kJ minimum
REQFirst aid kit
REQSurvival blanket or bivy
REQWhistle

Mega
Munter

48–50km  |  2,300m vert  |  5–10hrs

The Mega adds the East Whakanui and Mckerrow tracks, Clay Ridge, and another river crossing to the standard course. You must have completed the 32km Munter or an equivalent race within 24 months to enter.
